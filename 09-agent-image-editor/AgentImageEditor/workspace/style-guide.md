# Image Style Guide

All generated images must conform to these visual requirements.

## Style
- Monochrome pencil sketch — concept art aesthetic
- Black and white only, no color
- Loose gestural linework
- Visible hatching and cross-hatching for shading
- Early ideation / rough concept stage feel

## Composition
- Subject centered with approximately 20% margin padding on all sides
- Pure white background — no paper texture, no smudges, no dirt
- No decorative borders or frames

## Restrictions
- No text, no letters, no words anywhere in the image

## Default Settings
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Resolution target: 2k

## Prompt Suffixes
Append the following to every generation prompt:
"concept art sketch, black and white pencil drawing, loose gestural linework, hatching and cross-hatching, rough ideation stage, pure white background, no text, no color"
